About "The TechClasher"

Join us for innovation, coding, and transformation

The TechClasher is a Hackathon event that is organized by MANAN Club of Department of CSE - AI/ AI-DS bringing together students, professionals, and innovators to solve real-world challenges through technology. This intensive hackathon provides a platform for creative minds to showcase their skills, learn from industry experts, and compete for exciting prizes.
